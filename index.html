House Rules & Resident Conduct

Safety & Timings

  • While we offer flexible gate timings, residents returning after 10:00 PM are requested to inform the warden for security purposes[cite: 93, 100].
  • Visitors are allowed in the common areas/sit-out only between 10:00 AM and 7:00 PM[cite: 43].
  • Male visitors are strictly not permitted inside the rooms at any time[cite: 65, 100].

Hygiene & Maintenance

  • Residents are responsible for the cleanliness of their own rooms and wardrobes[cite: 90, 93].
  • Self-cleaning facilities in the kitchen must be used responsibly[cite: 93].
  • Waste should be segregated and disposed of in designated bins only.

Electricity & Assets

  • Switch off all fans, lights, and appliances when leaving the room.
  • Any damage to hostel property (beds, cupboards, furniture) will be charged to the resident[cite: 39, 41].
  • High-power appliances like induction stoves or heaters are not permitted in rooms.
