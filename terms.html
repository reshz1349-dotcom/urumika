1. Remote Booking Process

If you are unable to visit the property immediately, you can opt for our remote booking facility:

  1. Share your details via the website.
  2. Our team will guide you to select the ideal room category.
  3. Pay the token amount to confirm the booking.
  4. Visit the property to finalize your specific room.
  5. Pay the balance amount to complete the onboarding process.

2. Offers and Discounts

By selecting 'Reserve Now', you may unlock assured discounts on payment of a refundable 'Fee'. Upon successful onboarding, this discount is applied to your first month's rent. If you decide not to join after visiting, you may claim a refund of the Fee.

3. Payment Terms

Payments shall be settled within 30 days. We do not engage in unsolicited communication asking for sensitive personal banking information. Please verify authenticity before initiating any payment.
