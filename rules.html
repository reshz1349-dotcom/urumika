Resident Code of Conduct

To ensure a harmonious living environment, all residents must adhere to the following guidelines.

Noise & Conduct

  • Making loud noises or playing music between 10:30 PM to 6:00 AM is strictly prohibited.
  • Vandalism is a serious offence. Residents are liable for costs incurred due to destruction of property.
  • Possession or distribution of illegal substances is strictly prohibited.

Operations & Visitors

  • Visitors: Male visitors are not allowed in rooms. They may visit common areas between 10:00 AM and 7:00 PM.
  • Keys: Residents must keep keys safe. Loss of keys will incur a replacement charge.
  • Gate Timings: Flexible timings are offered, but late entry must be notified for security.

Hygiene & Maintenance

  • Cooking is allowed only in the pantry. Personal food must be labeled in the refrigerator.
  • Switch off all lights, fans, and ACs when leaving the room.
  • Use of high-power appliances (heaters/iron boxes) inside the room is prohibited.
